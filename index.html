She-Ra: Princess of Power

About She-Ra

She-Ra: Princess of Power is He-Man's twin sister. Born on Planet Eternia she is kidnapped at birth by Hordak and taken to Etheria to serve as a mind-controlled Horde. He-Man rescues her and brings her back to her parents in Eternia. However she decides to return to Etheria to lead the Great Rebellion.

She-Ra's Characteristics

  1. She's cooler than He-Man
  2. She has a winged unicorn called Swift Wind
  3. She wears the same clothes every day but never smells

She-Ra's Enemies

  • Hordak: The tyrannical ruler of Etheria.
  • Leech: An energy-draining creature with suction cup hands.
  • Imp: A shape-shifting spy who can disguise himself as everyday objects
  • Catra: The Force Captain of the Evil Horde who can transform into a panther.
